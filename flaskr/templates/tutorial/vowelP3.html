Now that you’ve played around with the chart, let’s unpack what the axes mean.

The horizontal axis represents where the tip of the tongue is in the mouth, either near the front of the mouth or the back.

Try recording the word "feed" or “hat”. They will show up on the left side of the chart, since they are fronted vowels.
